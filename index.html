About

Welcome to Hitu Tech! I'm Gaurav, a dedicated professional offering reliable computer repair and custom PC building services in Mumbai. With a passion for technology and years of industry experience, I provide personalized solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.

Whether you're facing hardware malfunctions, software glitches, or in need of a custom-built PC, I'm here to assist you. I understand the importance of your computer in your personal and professional life, which is why I prioritize fast and efficient repairs, minimizing any downtime.In addition to my repair services, I specialize in creating custom-built PCs that perfectly align with your requirements. From casual users to gamers and professionals, I can design and assemble a system that exceeds your expectations.

Convenience is key, which is why I offer at-your-doorstep service. No need to worry about transporting your computer or waiting in long queues. I come to you, to provide a hassle-free and convenient experience.

Contact me today to schedule an appointment or to learn more about how I can assist you with your computer needs. I look forward to serving you and providing you with top-quality computer repair and custom PC building services.
